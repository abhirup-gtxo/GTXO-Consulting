The problem

Your SEO playbook was written for 2019.

Google's AI Overviews answer the query without the click. ChatGPT search has 600M+ weekly users and never sends most of them. The old SEO playbook ships volume into a smaller and smaller pipe.

  • AI Overviews now appear on 60%+ of informational queries — and they cite, not link.
  • Half of your bottom-of-funnel pages aren't structured for LLM citation.
  • Editorial calendars optimise for keywords nobody clicks on anymore.
  • Nobody on the team is measuring whether ChatGPT mentions your brand.

Our take

Design content to be quoted, not just ranked.

We design pages with structured answers, named entities, and citation-friendly formatting. The LLMs read it, summarise it, and credit you when they answer.

Programmatic where it scales. Editorial where it earns attention. Technical SEO underneath both, so the foundation never breaks.

What we ship

Six things we build, in this order.

Topical map

The full surface area of queries your buyer asks. Mapped to intent, citation-readiness, and existing coverage.

Entity-rich pages

Named entities, structured Q&A blocks, schema markup. The format LLMs scan to find a citable answer.

Programmatic systems

Templates for high-leverage page systems — comparisons, integrations, locations, glossary. Hundreds of pages, none of them slop.

Technical foundation

Core Web Vitals, crawl efficiency, internal linking. The boring stuff that lets the smart stuff work.

Citation tracking

Custom monitor that pings when ChatGPT, Perplexity or Gemini mention your brand. Reported weekly.

Quarterly refresh

Algorithms move. We re-audit every page system every 90 days and fix what's drifted.

How we run it

A 90-day cycle. Built to compound.

Audit + topical map

Days 1–10. Crawl, content review, LLM-citation baseline, competitive gaps. Out: a topical map and the three highest-leverage page systems.

Ship the foundation

Days 10–30. Technical fixes, schema, the first programmatic system live. We're after indexation speed, not volume.

Editorial wave

Days 30–60. 12–20 pillar pieces, structured for citation. Each one briefed for LLM-readability, not just keywords.

Measure + scale

Days 60–90. Citation reports, ranking deltas, assisted pipeline. Kill what isn't working, scale what is.
