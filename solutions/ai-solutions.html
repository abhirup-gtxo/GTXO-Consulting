How we run it

From idea to live in 72 hours.

Day 0: scoping call

30 minutes. We define the problem in one sentence, name the data sources, decide what we won't build. Most projects scope inside a single call.

Day 1: spec + design

We write a 1-page spec, design the UI (or pick the stack), confirm with you. No multi-week design phase.

Day 2: build

Senior builder, modern stack, AI-accelerated. Most tools ship inside a working day. Landing pages are faster.

Day 3: ship + iterate

Live behind your domain, behind your auth, in your stack. We sit on it for a week to fix anything that surfaces.
