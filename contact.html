Before you ask

Common questions

How much does GTXO Consulting cost?

Engagements typically start at USD 1,500/month. That said, every scope is different — the right investment depends on your channels, goals, and the size of the opportunity. Book a call and we'll put together a customised proposal once we understand your requirements.

How long is a typical engagement?

Most clients start with a 90-day sprint to validate the channel and build the playbook, then roll into a monthly retainer. We don't lock you into 12-month contracts. The minimum commitment is 60 days — enough time to see real signal, not just vanity metrics.

What don't you do?

We don't do brand awareness for its own sake, content mills, or social media management that isn't tied to pipeline. We're a growth team, not a marketing agency. If your brief is "more followers" or "viral reach" without a revenue goal attached, we're probably not the right fit.

How quickly can we get started?

After the strategy call, we send a scoped proposal within 48 hours. If it's a fit, onboarding takes about one week — we run a kick-off session, audit your existing funnel, and have the first sprint plan in your hands before day 10.
