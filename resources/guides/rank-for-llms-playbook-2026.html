The Rank-for-LLMs playbook (2026 edition)

What we changed in our SEO process the moment ChatGPT became a search engine.

Guide · 24 min SEOAI

Why LLM citation is the new first-page ranking

In 2024, ChatGPT passed 100M daily active users conducting search-like queries. By 2026, more B2B buying research happens inside LLMs than on Google for several verticals. If your brand isn't cited, you don't exist to that buyer.

The three pillars

1. Entity authority

LLMs are trained on the web but reason about entities — named things with attributes and relationships. Build entity-rich content that states facts clearly: who you are, what you do, proof points with numbers.

2. Topical coverage

You need dense, interlinked coverage of your category. One great article isn't enough. LLMs favor sources that cover a topic comprehensively from multiple angles.

3. Citation surface

Get cited in places LLMs trust: Reddit threads, product forums, LinkedIn, industry publications. These are the citation surfaces that feed training data and retrieval systems.
