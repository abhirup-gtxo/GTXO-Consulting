GUIDE

The 90-day GTM checklist for Series A

Every milestone, every miss-and-fix-it pattern we've seen.

Guide · 16 min GTM

The GTM clock starts at term sheet

Series A investors expect marketing to be a growth lever within 90 days. Most founders wait too long to hire, then hire wrong. This checklist fixes that.

Week 1–2: Input

Interview 20 customers. Listen for the exact words they use to describe their problem. Don't invent language — steal theirs. Map your ICP scoring rubric before you touch the website.

Week 3–4: Write

Positioning narrative. One page. If it takes more than one page you don't have clarity yet. Ship v1 to your best customer and ask: does this sound like you?

Week 5–8: Ship

Website, outbound sequence, one paid channel. Not all three at once — pick the highest-signal channel for your ICP and go deep. Measure weekly.

Week 9–12: Scale

Double down on what's working. Fire the channels that aren't by week eight — don't let them limp to week twelve.
