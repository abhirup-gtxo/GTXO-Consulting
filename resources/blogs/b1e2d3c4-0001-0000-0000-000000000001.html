Why we kill marketing strategy docs after week two

The first 60 days decide the year. Here’s the cadence we use to ship them.

Essay · 8 min GTMProcess

Most strategy documents die in a shared Google Drive folder, never opened after week two. We stopped writing them that way.

What we do instead

We ship a one-page operating doc on day one, then update it weekly based on what the data is telling us. No more 40-slide decks that are out of date before the ink is dry.

The first 60 days are about learning, not executing a plan you wrote before you knew anything. Quick wins first — they fund the trust you need for the bigger moves.
