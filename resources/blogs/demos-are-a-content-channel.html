Demos are a content channel — start treating them that way

The unsung pipeline lever for B2B SaaS in 2026.

Essay · 6 min ContentSales

Every demo your team gives is a compressed, high-intent version of your best content. Most companies let it disappear into a Zoom recording that nobody watches.

Record, clip, distribute

We’ve seen clients 3× their demo-sourced pipeline by simply clipping the best 90 seconds of each call and posting it. The objection-handling sequence is content. The product walkthrough is content. The customer question is content.
