CASE STUDY

Cutting CAC by 41% before scaling spend

Creative engine + LP rebuild. We fixed the maths first.

D2C beverages · 4 months Paid

The situation

A D2C beverages brand was scaling spend on Meta but burning cash — CAC at $2,100, LTV at $1,800. They wanted more budget. We said: fix the maths first.

What we did

We audited 180 creative assets, rebuilt the LP from scratch around a single conversion goal, and set up server-side attribution so we could actually trust the data. Then we launched a structured creative testing cadence.

Results

CAC dropped from $2,100 to $1,240 in four months. Only then did we scale spend — 3× budget, 3.6× pipeline.
