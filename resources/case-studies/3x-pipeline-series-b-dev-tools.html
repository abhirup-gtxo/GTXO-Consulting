CASE STUDY

How we 3× pipeline for a Series B dev-tools company

Programmatic SEO + outbound automation. From 40 demos/mo to 138.

B2B SaaS · 6 months SEOOutbound

The situation

A Series B dev-tools company was stuck at 40 demos/month. They had strong product-market fit but marketing-sourced pipeline was thin. Sales was relying on founder relationships.

What we did

We built a programmatic SEO system targeting 2,400 long-tail keywords in the developer tooling space, combined with an outbound motion using Clay + Apollo. Within 90 days we had the first organic citations from ChatGPT and Perplexity.

Results

After six months: 138 demos/month (up from 40), +312% marketing-sourced pipeline, $684k in closed revenue attributed to the content engine.
