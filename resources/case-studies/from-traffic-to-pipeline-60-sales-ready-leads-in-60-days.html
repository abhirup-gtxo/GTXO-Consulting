CASE STUDY

From Traffic to Pipeline: 60 Sales-Ready Leads in 60 Days

How Gushwork.ai turned stalled top-of-funnel traffic into a pipeline of Founders, CEOs, and Directors through precision LinkedIn Ads across 5 geographies in two months. Channel: LinkedIn Ads (Lead Gen) Duration: Oct - Dec 2024 (60 days) Industry: B2B

B2B ABM

Good product. Great traffic. Zero pipeline.


Gushwork.ai, an AI-first platform built for staffing and recruitment agencies, was seeing solid website traffic, but conversions weren't keeping up. Most visitors were still in the awareness or consideration stage, interested, but not quite ready to take the next step.


That's where GTXO Consulting stepped in. Over 60 days we ran a highly targeted LinkedIn Ads programme, constantly testing and refining campaigns across geographies, audiences, and creatives. The focus was simple: reach real decision-makers and drive meaningful conversations.



Four compounding problems blocking pipeline growth



Our approach: built to test fast, scale what worked, kill what didn't


The playbook had three phases: define the audience precisely, launch structured multi-geo campaigns, then iterate aggressively based on CPL performance, pausing losers within days, doubling down on winners.






Month 1, October 2024: experimental India campaign


Before scaling budget or geography, we ran a single experimental campaign targeting India exclusively to validate the core hypothesis, calibrate CPL expectations, and test creative messaging with the ICP. It delivered 25 leads at $29 CPL in 30 days. All decision-makers. 30,357 impressions with a 1.06% CTR.


Month 2, November 2024: full geo scale-up


Budget scaled 4x and expanded into US, UK, Canada, and ANZ alongside India. Seven campaigns ran simultaneously. The broader geo mix raised the blended CPL to $88, expected at scale, while delivering 35 additional leads, all decision-makers. 5 geographies running simultaneously across India, US, UK, Canada, and ANZ. Every lead was a Founder, CEO, MD, or Director.


Lead quality: every single lead was a decision-maker



Key learnings: what worked and what we cut


What worked: phone capture over email-only for India. Founder/CEO messaging converts best. Rapid A/B testing to find winning angles. ICP-fit leads convert to pipeline faster.


What we optimised: low-ROI geos killed within 2 weeks. Budget reallocated to best performers. Bid optimization to reduce CPL. Dormant leads moved to nurture tracks.


A proven playbook for B2B pipeline on LinkedIn


In 60 days, Gushwork.ai went from a top-heavy funnel with no paid engine to 60 sales-ready leads, every one of them a C-suite or Director-level buyer at a recruitment or staffing firm. The CPL dropped 50% month-over-month as we cut losing campaigns and scaled winning creatives.


The result validates a clear formula: precise ICP targeting + structured A/B testing + ruthless budget reallocation = a scalable, predictable pipeline engine on LinkedIn.
