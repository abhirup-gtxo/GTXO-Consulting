What we hear, constantly

Referral growth has a flat line.

Partner network grew the firm for a decade. It can't grow it for another. The pipeline conversation in the partner meeting is the same one as last year — and the year before.

  • Inbound is referral-only. Growth tracks the partner network's stamina.
  • Brand sounds like every other firm in the category. Differentiation lives in proposals, not in market.
  • Thought leadership exists, but doesn't convert to booked calls.
  • LinkedIn is patchy. Partners post inconsistently. No system.
  • No infrastructure to repackage client work into authority assets.

Our take

Authority is a system, not a personality.

We treat partner authority as content infrastructure: each partner has a content cadence, each cadence has a repackaging plan, each piece has a distribution motion. The system runs whether or not anyone feels inspired this month.

Sales Navigator outbound, partner-amplification on LinkedIn, and a small library of authority pieces compound into booked calls. We don't gate anything — gating works against you in this category.
